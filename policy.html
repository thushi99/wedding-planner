Privacy Policy

Dreams Wedding Planners is committed to protecting user privacy and using our technology in the safest way. We understand that all users of our web site are quite rightly concerned to know that their data will not be used for any purpose unintended by them, and will not accidentally fall into the hands of a third party. Our policy is both specific and strict. It complies with the United States law [and with the laws of all jurisdictions of which we are aware]. If you think our policy falls short of your expectations or that we are failing to abide by our policy, do please tell us.

We regret that if there are one or more points below with which you are not happy, your only recourse is to please leave our web site. Except as set out below, we do not share, or sell, or disclose to a third party, any personally identifiable information collected at this site.

How We Collect Your Information

The first thing to remember about privacy is that there is only so much information that any website can collect without your answering of specific questions. For example, in order to post any content on the Dreams Wedding Planners Website, you must first register. During the registration process you are required to provide certain personally identifiable information, such as your e-mail address.

User Content

Any content (comments and reviews) you post on the Dreams Wedding Planners Website becomes public. By submitting any comments and reviews, this information may be collected and used by others. User Content may be also used by Dreams Wedding Planners as described in our Terms of Service. We are not responsible for any personally identifiable information that you choose to include in such User Content. You understand and acknowledge that, even after removal, copies of User Content may remain viewable in cached and archived pages or if other users have copied or stored your User Content.

Use of your Personal Information

Your registration is handled through dreamsweddingplanners.com and subsequently, all of your information is stored and used through their technology.

  • 1. Dreams Wedding Planners collects and uses your personal information to operate the Dreams Wedding Planners Website and deliver the services you have requested. Dreams Wedding Planners also uses your personally identifiable information to inform you of other products or services available from Dreams Wedding Planners and its affiliates.

  • 2. Dreams Wedding Planners does not sell, rent or lease its user lists to third parties

  • 3. Dreams Wedding Planners will use any data collected to enforce our Terms of Use, to comply with legal obligations, act under exigent circumstances to protect the personal safety of users of Dreams Wedding Planners or the public, and otherwise manage our business.

  • 4. Dreams Wedding Planners will only use and share your information as described in this policy.

  • 5. Dreamsweddingplanners.com does not sell, rent or lease its user lists to third parties. Dreamsweddingplanners.com will only use and share your information as described in this policy.
